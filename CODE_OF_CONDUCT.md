# Code of Conduct

We follow a simple set of expectations to keep the community safe and productive.

## Our pledge
We strive to provide a harassment‑free, inclusive experience for everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity/expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity/orientation.

## Standards
- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussion on technical topics; avoid personal attacks, trolling, or harassment.
- Use welcoming language; no discriminatory remarks or imagery.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal insults.
- Public or private intimidation or stalking.
- Publishing others’ private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement
- Instances can be reported privately via the channels in `SECURITY.md`.
- Maintainers may warn, temporarily suspend, or permanently ban contributors for violations.
- Severe incidents may be escalated to the contributor’s employer or platform administrators where appropriate.

## Scope
This code applies to all project spaces: repository issues/PRs, discussions, chats, and in-person events when representing the project.
